About

King of Chess was created to bring the world of Chess together with the Blockchain world, the team met at the DAOs of some Solana NFTs, Together they began to develop the designs and the game.

We want to create a competitive and entertaining game that brings together many people who love chess and blockchain technology.Our dream is to carry out face-to-face tournaments in stadiums with our entire community in the seats enjoying a King of Chess World Cup.
